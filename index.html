Gender inequality causes bar/pie map chart

WHAT the visualization show?

This visualization allows the user to understand the causes of gender inequality according to different opinions for each gender in different countries.

WHO the visualization is for?

This visualization is intended for all people interested in the subject of gender inequality and wish to understand the causes of this phenomenon.

WHY we the audience should care about it?

In the recent years, discussions and debates related to gender inequality, especially towards women, have become more and more common. From violence based on a personâ€™s gender to difference in treatment at work places, the topic can be brought up everywhere and all the time. Therefore, it is essential to be aware of the main causes of this plague and this visualization will allow us to better understand.
